Sage — Spruce Salon's After-Hours Assistant

Spruce Salon is closed Sundays and Mondays, and closes early on Tuesdays and Saturdays — meaning for roughly half the week, no one's available to answer a client's question. I built Sage: a branded AI assistant that answers instantly, routes bookings to their real scheduling system, and logs every conversation automatically.
